About Us

Founded in 2021 by three industry professionals with a shared passion for dependable logistics, G&S Transport and Logistics has grown into a trusted partner for businesses across Victoria. With a modern fleet of over 40 vehicles—from nimble vans to robust B-doubles—we specialize in safe, on-time deliveries across Melbourne and regional areas.

Our success is built on one simple principle: people first. Our experienced drivers are the backbone of our operations, committed to excellence with every delivery.

Our Vision

To become one of Australia’s most reliable and cost-effective logistics providers— where adaptability, integrity, and performance meet. We aim to build lasting partnerships by exceeding expectations, one delivery at a time.
